### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**

The working environment at FCAJ is very friendly, open, and dynamic. Team members are always willing to support and guide me whenever I face difficulties, even outside official working hours. The workplace is well-organized and comfortable, creating motivation and helping me maintain high concentration. In addition, if the company organizes short social activities or regular team bonding sessions, members will have more opportunities to connect and understand each other better.

**2. Support from Mentor and Team Admin**

I was fortunate to receive very dedicated support from my mentor. My mentor always provided detailed guidance, explained complex issues clearly, and encouraged me to think critically by asking questions. Instead of giving direct answers, the mentor created opportunities for me to explore and solve problems on my own, which made me more proactive at work. In addition, the Team Admin members also provided very thoughtful support regarding procedures and documents, making the onboarding and daily work process run smoothly.

**3. Fit Between Work and Academic Background**

The assigned tasks were highly compatible with the Software Engineering major I am studying at university. The internship not only helped me strengthen and apply theory in practice, but also exposed me to many new areas of technology. This was an excellent stepping stone in bridging the gap between academic learning and real business practice.

**4. Learning Opportunities and Skill Development**

The internship brought significant growth in both technical and soft skills. I had the opportunity to learn about professional project management processes, practice teamwork effectively, and improve professional communication in a corporate environment. The practical experience and guidance from my mentor are invaluable assets for my future career.

**5. Corporate Culture and Team Spirit**

FCAJ’s culture stood out because of the mutual respect, seriousness at work, and the cheerful, connected atmosphere. When projects reached critical stages, the team spirit became especially evident: everyone worked together, supported one another unconditionally, and there was no distinction between full-time employees and interns. This made me feel truly part of the team.

**6. Policies and Benefits for Interns**

The company has a clear support policy for interns, which greatly motivates them. In addition, the leadership’s flexibility regarding working hours and the opportunity to participate in quality internal training sessions were major advantages, showing genuine care for the development of young talent.

### Other Questions

- What are you most satisfied with during your internship?
==> What I am most satisfied with is the mentoring approach at FCAJ and the supportive culture. Instead of simply assigning tasks and waiting for results or giving direct answers, my mentor guided me to think independently, explore, and solve problems. This trust and freedom helped me become much more confident in self-learning and handling real-world challenges.

- What do you think the company should improve for future interns?
==> I suggest that the company organize more networking and small teambuilding activities between interns and FCAJ members. This proactive connection would help future interns open up more easily, learn more from senior colleagues, and feel like a genuine part of the team.

- If you recommend it to your friends, would you advise them to intern here? Why?
==> Absolutely yes. FCAJ is an ideal environment for students who want to experience real working conditions. It offers not only well-structured workflows and practical projects aligned with their field, but also colleagues who respect, listen, and are willing to share their experience, helping interns develop both technical and soft skills comprehensively.
